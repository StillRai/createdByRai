The Journey


Welcome to my tech journey — a series of happy accidents and questionable decisions! It all started back in 2003 when I was voluntold into web design by Mom (thanks, Mom!). From there, I made the oh-so-wise choice of Joomla over WordPress, because why make life easy?

I then spent countless hours mastering Flash for web development (RIP, my masterpiece), and decided to dabble in PHP to alter Joomla templates — just enough to get the job done. During my time working as an underwriter at HSBC, tech remained my constant hobby. Throughout various customer service roles, tech was always there in the background. It's been a wild ride, and who knows what tech mishap I'll stumble into next?
